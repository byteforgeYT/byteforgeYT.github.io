Frequently Asked Questions

What is Taskination?

Taskination is a platform that connects freelancers with clients through bounties.

What are Bounties?

Bounties are tasks posted by clients for freelancers to complete. Clients can set thier own prices and choose from a variety of qualified freelancers.

Is Taskination free?

Yes! Taskination is completely free to use. However, in order to create a bounty, you must deposit the costs upfront to help minimize fraud.
